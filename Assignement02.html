Exploratory analyses

The ToothGrowth dataset the response in the length of odontoblasts (teeth) in each of 10 guinea pigs at each of three dose levels of Vitamin C (0.5, 1, and 2 mg) with each of two delivery methods (orange juice or ascorbic acid). The data frame is composed of 60 observations on 3 variables:
- len: numeric Tooth length
- supp: factor Supplement type (VC for abosrbis acid, OJ for orange juice)
- dose: numeric Dose in milligrams
